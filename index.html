to read legal document



Convert your boring-flat-long legal document into more user-friendly format and ensure your service's user reads your legal document, e.g. Terms and Conditions, Software License, Privacy Policy, etc.

LegalReader is a jQuery plugin.

1. Write in Markdown

Write your legal document in markdown format and follow our writing conventions, so Legal Reader can process your document. Read the conventions.
